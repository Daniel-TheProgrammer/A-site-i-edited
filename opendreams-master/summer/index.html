About Us

At Open Dreams, we see education as a gift that opens doors. We help those who need it most find the quality education they deserve

Quality education is the sine qua non of our survival; Giving every young person the opportunity to access quality education is therefore a shared responsibility.~~ Elvis Tanwie

Mission

We strongly believe each person is destined to do great things in life regardless of their background. Our dream is to open doors and help them do it

Vision

Work with High Achieving Low Income students who dare dream big to study abroad on fully funded scholarships.

Passion

Providing essential ingredients - computers, internet access, study guides, teachers and a classroom - needed to amximize educational opportunities
